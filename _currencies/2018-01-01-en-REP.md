	                      <h2>{{page.coin_a_name}}'s Features</h2>
	                    </div>
	                      <div class="panel-content">
	                       Augur aims to revolutionize the prediction market, and to do so, change the way people receive and verify 'truth.' The main idea is to make more exact predictions of larger groups rather than a small group of experts. Also, People can set events on any topic and buy shares for the possible outcomes of those events. Ether and bitcoins are accepted on the platform. Algorithms will then work with a wide range of information and provide consensus-based top predictions. Once the event is signed, the funds attached to the share of the exact result are distributed to the 'winner'.
