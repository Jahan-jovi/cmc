	                      <h2>{{page.coin_a_name}}'s Features</h2>
	                    </div>
	                      <div class="panel-content">
	                       Sia uses a new cryptocurrency, called siacoin. The most important features of Sia can only be accessed using siacoins. All storage contracts and all Sia payment channels require the possession of siacoins. This requirement means that when Sia grows in use, there will also be demand for siacoins. As demand increases, prices will increase. If Sia is being used for billions of dollars of storage, billions of dollars of siacoins will be required. The value of siacoin attached cannot be separated from the amount of storage being used on the Sia network.
