	                       Steem is a proof of work currency with a scheduled block time of 3 seconds. Steem PoW mining is made within 63 seconds of 21 miners (witnesses). 19 of the miners were voted in advance, the other one had the most computational power, and the last one was chosen from a queue of witnesses not among the top 19 elected witnesses. 90% of the block reward is given to the funding to reward curators and PoW miners, while the remaining 10% is liquid in the form of steal and is used to reward content creators.
