### Description

<p>StableCoin is a new type of decentralized currency. SBC has what it calls the optimised block time of 40 seconds - to play the balance between orphaned blocks and transaction confirmation time - i.e. Forking issues. Difficulty is retargetted every 90 blocks and the coin reward is set to 25 coins after block 900. They have plans for their own ecommerce site.</p>
